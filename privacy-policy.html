Privacy Policy
Pointnode Ltd · Last updated April 2025

This Privacy Policy covers all craneIQ products and applications developed and operated by Pointnode Ltd, including the craneIQ mobile app and any associated cloud services. We handle personal data responsibly and in line with the UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Who We Are

Pointnode Ltd is the data controller for all personal data processed through craneIQ products and services. We are based in the West Midlands, United Kingdom.

Contact: joe@pointnode.io · craneiq.co.uk

2. What craneIQ Is

craneIQ is a platform of products for overhead crane technology — including condition monitoring, safety enforcement, automation, load tracking and cloud visibility. The platform includes a mobile application, cloud dashboard and hardware installed on overhead cranes. This policy covers all of these.

3. Data We Collect

Account and Identity Data

  • Name and email address
  • Job role and employer organisation
  • Training certifications and expiry dates (where applicable to your product)
  • Operator assignments — which cranes or sites you are authorised to access

Operational and Inspection Data

  • Pre-use inspection records — responses, timestamps, pass/fail outcomes
  • Photos and voice notes attached to inspection items or defect reports
  • Crane unlock, lock and session records
  • Defect reports and their resolution status
  • Engineer lockout and tagout records

Crane and Monitoring Data

  • Load data — individual lift weights, load spectrum
  • Design working period (DWP) — hoist, brake and controls
  • Vibration data, motor temperatures, running hours
  • Starts, brakings, E-stops and over-temperature events
  • Crane identifier and installation details

Device and Technical Data

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • Session timestamps and connectivity status

Location Data

The app may record device location at the time an inspection is submitted, where your device permissions allow. This is associated with inspection records for audit purposes. You can manage location access in your device settings.

5. Data Sharing

Within Your Organisation

Inspection records, crane data, operator assignments and access logs are visible to authorised personnel within your organisation — typically site managers, supervisors and engineering teams. The scope is configured by your administrator.

Pointnode Ltd

As platform operator, Pointnode Ltd can access data for technical support, maintenance and investigating reported safety issues. We do not use your data for our own commercial or marketing purposes.

Third-Party Infrastructure

We use trusted third-party providers to host and operate the craneIQ backend. They act as data processors under our instruction and are bound by appropriate data processing agreements. We do not sell personal data.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose data where required by law or to cooperate with regulatory investigations — including those under LOLER, PUWER or the Health and Safety at Work Act 1974.

6. Data Retention

Inspection records, audit logs and defect reports are kept for a minimum of two years in line with LOLER requirements. Your administrator may configure longer retention. Account data is kept while your account is active and for a reasonable period after closure to support compliance obligations.

7. Data Security

We use appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (TLS) between the app and our servers
  • Multi-tenant data isolation — your organisation's data is kept separate at database level
  • Role-based access controls
  • Append-only audit log — inspection and access records cannot be modified once written

In the event of a breach likely to affect your rights and freedoms, we will notify the ICO within 72 hours and affected individuals as required.

8. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict or port your personal data, and to object to processing. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at joe@pointnode.io. We will respond within one month.

Note that some rights are limited where we have a legal obligation to retain data — for example, LOLER inspection records.

If you are not satisfied with our response you can complain to the Information Commissioner's Office: ico.org.uk · 0303 123 1113.

9. Cookies and Analytics

The craneIQ app does not use cookies. The craneiq.co.uk website uses minimal analytics. No advertising or tracking cookies are used on any craneIQ property.

10. Children

craneIQ products are intended for use by adults in industrial workplaces. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

11. International Transfers

Our infrastructure is within the UK and/or European Economic Area. Where data is transferred outside these regions, appropriate safeguards are in place under UK GDPR.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated to administrators. Continued use of craneIQ products after an updated policy is published constitutes acceptance.
