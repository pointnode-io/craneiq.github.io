Nature of the Service and Safety Obligations

Important: The craneIQ Service, including the Guard Feature, is an operational management and access control platform. It is not a primary safety system and does not replace, replicate or override the existing hardwired safety functions of any crane. The crane's existing emergency stop circuit, safety relays and protective devices remain fully independent of the craneIQ Platform at all times. In all emergency situations, operators must use the crane's hardwired emergency stop and follow their facility's established emergency procedures.

craneIQ Guard — Access Control Feature

The Guard Feature provides pre-use inspection enforcement and operator access control. It operates by enabling or disabling a control circuit that supplements the crane's existing control architecture. The Guard Feature:

  • Can restrict access to the crane's operating controls until a pre-use inspection has been completed and passed
  • Cannot initiate, command or cause any crane motion
  • Cannot disable, bypass or override any existing hardwired safety function, emergency stop circuit or protective device on the crane
  • Does not form part of the crane's safety-rated control system as defined under applicable machinery safety standards
  • Operates as an additional access control layer only — existing safety functions take precedence under all circumstances
