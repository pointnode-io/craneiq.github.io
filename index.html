craneIQ uses open architecture that works with any manufacturer's crane and controls. Your data is yours — accessible in open formats by your engineers and third-party maintenance teams without manufacturer involvement.
02
Proven in the harshest environments
Galvanizing plants, steel mills, foundries, automotive lines — craneIQ has been running reliably in some of the most demanding industrial environments for years. Not just rated for it. Proven in it.
03
Removes human error from crane operations
craneIQ Antisway and Automation remove the dependency on operator experience. Inexperienced operators perform as consistently as your most experienced from day one — and automated cycles repeat precisely every time.
04
Plan maintenance — don't react to breakdowns
craneIQ Core and Monitoring track DWP, brake condition and load history continuously — counting down from 100% based on actual usage. Know when components are due for service before they fail.
05

craneIQ Vision has stopped real snag and side pull events on working cranes. craneIQ Zoning prevents collisions with buildings, other cranes and floor equipment automatically — without relying on operator awareness.

Industries
Built for demanding
industrial environments.
craneIQ products are proven across a broad range of heavy and process industries — anywhere overhead cranes work hard in challenging conditions.
Galvanizing
Acid fumes, high heat and continuous operation. craneIQ Core and LoadTrack are proven in galvanizing environments — tracking dip cycles, load data and crane health automatically.
Steel & Metals
Heavy loads, dusty environments and demanding duty cycles. craneIQ Monitoring tracks DWP and load spectrum across steel mill and stockholder cranes of any manufacturer.
Automotive Manufacturing
Precise load placement, consistent cycle times and safety in busy facilities. craneIQ Automation and Antisway remove human error from repetitive crane movements on production lines.
Waste to Energy
Process cycle logging and reliable automated operation. craneIQ LoadTrack logs every feed cycle automatically — replacing manual records with accurate production data.
Foundries & Castings
Extreme temperatures and harsh conditions that test equipment. craneIQ has been proven in foundry environments for years — monitoring crane health and protecting loads in difficult conditions.
General Manufacturing
From shipyards to paper mills, food production to heavy engineering — craneIQ products work on any overhead crane regardless of manufacturer, adding intelligence without disruption.
