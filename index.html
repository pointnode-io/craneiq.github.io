01
No proprietary lock-in
craneIQ products use open architecture that works with any manufacturer's controls. Your engineers can interrogate and maintain the system without specialist support or manufacturer permission.
02
Proven in the harshest environments
Galvanizing plants, steel mills, foundries, automotive lines — craneIQ has been running reliably in some of the most demanding industrial environments for years. Not just rated for it. Proven in it.
03
Operators perform immediately
Operators with limited training can move loads with dramatically less sway, position accurately, and work safely from day one. craneIQ Antisway and Automation remove the dependency on years of operator experience.
04
Keeps cranes running
craneIQ Monitoring gives engineering teams the data to diagnose problems early — before unplanned downtime occurs. Every lift recorded, design life tracked, vibration monitored continuously.
05
Prevents incidents — proven
craneIQ Vision has stopped real snag and side pull events on working cranes. craneIQ Zoning prevents collisions with buildings, other cranes, and floor-level equipment automatically.
