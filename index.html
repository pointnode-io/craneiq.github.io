Why craneIQ
Built for the real world
of overhead cranes.
craneIQ products are developed by engineers with deep experience in overhead crane systems. Every feature solves a real operational problem.
01
Retrofit to any crane
All craneIQ products are designed to fit any overhead crane regardless of age or manufacturer. No crane replacement required — protect your existing investment.
02
No complex programming
craneIQ Automation uses a teach-and-replay system like robot programming. Drive the crane, record the path, replay on command. No specialist engineers needed.
03
Modular by design
Start with one product and add more over time. Core, Automation, Monitoring, and Cloud are designed to integrate seamlessly when deployed together.
04
Real-time cloud visibility
craneIQ Cloud puts live condition monitoring data in your browser or phone. Email and SMS alerts. Remote access for support and software updates — no site visit needed.
05
Protect cranes and people
From side pull prevention with craneIQ Vision to collision avoidance with craneIQ Zoning — craneIQ products are built around safety outcomes, not just features.
06
UK engineered, 24/7 supported
