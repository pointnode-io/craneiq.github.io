Overview

When something goes wrong with a crane, most operations find out when production stops. craneIQ Cloud means you find out before that happens. All craneIQ monitoring data feeds live to a cloud dashboard accessible from any browser or phone — whether you're on the shop floor, in an office, or at a different site entirely. Configure alerts so you're notified the moment a threshold is exceeded. View DWP status, load history, fault logs and usage trends without needing to access the crane itself. And when something needs attention, the craneIQ team can connect remotely to diagnose and resolve — no engineer travelling to site, no waiting, no unnecessary production interruption. For facility managers, it replaces speculative decisions with data-driven ones. For engineering teams, it means full crane visibility without the safety risk of accessing live equipment.

What it does for you
  • See live crane data without accessing the crane All monitoring data visible remotely — no need to climb to the crane, access high-voltage panels or stop production to retrieve data
  • Make data-driven decisions — not speculative ones When a problem arises, recorded events and trend data show exactly what happened and when — no guesswork about the cause
  • Email alerts the moment thresholds are exceeded Configure what matters — load limits, DWP percentage, motor temperatures — and get notified immediately, wherever you are
  • SMS alarms for critical events The most urgent alerts go straight to your phone as a text message — even if you're away from a desk
  • Remote diagnostics — no site visit required The craneIQ team connects remotely to diagnose faults and make adjustments — faster resolution, less downtime
  • Remote software updates System improvements deployed remotely — no engineer visit, no production interruption
  • Multi-site overview — all cranes, one dashboard Manage cranes across multiple facilities from a single view — ideal for plant managers overseeing several sites
  • 5-year data retention for compliance and audits Full historical record available for trend analysis, insurance, statutory inspection planning and compliance reporting
