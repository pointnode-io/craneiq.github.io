Overview

Load swing costs time, causes accidents, and puts enormous stress on cranes and structures. Traditionally, solving it has meant expensive sensor installations, specialist commissioning, and ongoing maintenance of complex measurement systems. craneIQ Antisway needs none of that. If your crane has Variable Frequency Drives on the travel motions — which most modern cranes do — craneIQ Antisway can be fitted and commissioned in less than a day. No sensors. No encoders. No cameras. The open-loop algorithm modifies the VFD speed profile to move the crane in a way that naturally suppresses swing. Operators with limited training immediately move loads with dramatically less sway — improving safety, productivity and cycle times.
