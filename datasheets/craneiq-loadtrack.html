Overview

In many industrial processes, the crane is the centre of production — but most plants have no automatic record of what it lifted, when, or how much. craneIQ LoadTrack changes that. Every load is automatically weighed and recorded. Live weights are shown on wireless display screens positioned wherever you need them in the factory — no cable runs, no fixed positions. For process industries like galvanizing, waste to energy, and scrap handling, LoadTrack also logs complete process cycles — recording what went into the process, how long it took, and what came out. The result is an automatic production record driven by the crane itself.
