Overview

Most overhead cranes run with no real visibility into how they are performing, how hard they are working, or how much usable life they have left. craneIQ Core changes that. It sits alongside your existing crane controls — adding a complete layer of intelligence that protects the crane, protects the load, and gives you the data you need to manage maintenance proactively rather than react to breakdowns. Every lift is recorded. Design life is tracked continuously. The brake and motor are monitored in real time. Shock loads and slack rope are detected and stopped before damage occurs. And unlike condition monitoring systems that only work on the crane brand they came with, craneIQ Core works on any overhead crane from any manufacturer — making it the right choice for mixed fleets, third-party cranes, and any installation where you don't want to be tied to one supplier's ecosystem. Proven in galvanizing plants, steel mills, foundries, automotive lines and recycling facilities for years.

What it does for you
  • Overload protection — stops at 110% of rated capacity A load cell measures every lift in real time. If the load exceeds 110% of rated capacity, hoisting is stopped before damage occurs
  • Tracks design life (DWP) — hoist, brake and controls separately Hoist DWP counts from 100% based on actual load cycles. Brake DWP is calculated separately from starts and E-stops. Controls DWP tracks contactor and relay life — the full picture, not just one number
  • Prevents slack rope, shock loads and side pull Intervenes automatically before abnormal forces damage the hoist — protecting the crane and the load
  • Records every lift from day one — no manual logging Complete load spectrum history stored on board. Full audit trail for inspections, insurance and compliance
  • Logs every start, braking, E-stop and over-temperature event A complete operational history — know exactly how your crane has been used and what events have occurred
  • Anti-sway on travel movements Dramatically reduces load swing — operators with limited training move loads safely and efficiently
  • Supports tandem hoist operations Monitor and synchronise two hoists working together on the same load safely
  • Works on any crane from any manufacturer Not tied to one brand's ecosystem — fits any overhead crane new or existing, regardless of who made it
